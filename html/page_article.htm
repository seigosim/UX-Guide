Host Rewards Program

You have a unique opportunity to become an Arbonne Host and earn exclusive rewards when you gather friends together to learn about Arbonne and experience our botanically based products. You and friends will have fun learning and shopping for products, and the more everyone shops, the more rewards you earn!

Oh, the rewards…

You can use host rewards to try new products or get your Arbonne favorites, the choice is yours! Arbonne Hosts are rewarded in four ways, based on the total Retail Volume (RV) for your Group Presentation and a minimum of three orders.

Here’s how it all works…

Terms and Conditions
  • A minimum of three orders with unique Arbonne IDs (whether Client, Preferred Client or Consultant) must be placed through the program for the Host to qualify for rewards. Host rewards cap at 1,250 RV.
  • Guest orders processed through the Host Rewards Program do not qualify for host rewards. Guest orders must be placed within seven days of the Group Presentation.
  • When a Right Start Value Pack (RSVP) is purchased through the Host Rewards Program, 350 RV will be attributed to the Total Group Presentation RV.
  • Host rewards can only be used toward regular price products and cannot be used in conjunction with other product promotions, such as Purchase with Purchase, New Consultant Free Product, Twice as Nice, RSVPs and Gift Sets. In addition, host rewards may not be used in conjunction with one another (for example, a Host cannot use the $125 for $25 reward to purchase the 50% discount reward product).
  • Host rewards must be redeemed in one order within 14 days of the Group Presentation. Unused host rewards will be forfeited and cannot be redeemed at a later date.
  • Standard tax rates apply. Standard shipping applies on all Group Presentation orders. If the Group Presentation total RV is 500 or more, the Host will receive free shipping for the products selected as the host rewards.
