Main Navigation

The Main Navigation menu allows direct access to many areas of the site. Guests see three top-level menu items (Shop, Company and Opportunity) and logged in Consultants see an additional Consultants menu item. Hovering the mouse over the main items switches the subnav bar. Hover over the subnav items to reveal the third-level navigation.

Timing of the menu dropdowns has been tuned to allow the mouse pointer to quickly cross over adjacent menu items without switching the submenus inadvertently.
